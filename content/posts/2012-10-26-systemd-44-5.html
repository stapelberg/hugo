--- layout: post date: 2012-10-26 19:32:00 +0200 title: "systemd 44-5 available" categories: Artikel tags: - debian ---

mbiebl has recently uploaded systemd 44-5 to Debian unstable. If you are already using systemd, you should definitely upgrade to this version, it fixes a lot of little annoyances. This is especially important if you are using libvirt >= 0.10.2-4 or if you experienced a hanging boot when your network cable is attached.
